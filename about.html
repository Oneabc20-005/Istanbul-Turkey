Istanbul, the largest city in Turkey. The city lies in both Europe and Asia with a population of over 15 million people. History, Turkish Istanbul, formerly Constantinople, ancient Byzantium, largest city and principal seaport of Turkey. It was the capital of both the Byzantine Empire and the Ottoman Empire. Facts, #1 Istanbul is the only city in the world that straddles two continents: Asia and Europe. #2 Under the Ottoman Empire, the city was renowned for having, 1400 public toilets and #3 The Grand Bazaar is the biggest old covered bazaar in the world, with over 3000 shops. Attractions, #1 Hagia Sophia, #2 Topkapi Palace and #3 Blue Mosque.
